# Prompt — Logo Renove GLP-1 (Nano Banana 2)

---

## PROMPT PRINCIPAL (cole no gerador)

```
Professional app logo for "Renove GLP-1", a premium mobile health app for people undergoing GLP-1 treatment (Tirzepatide/Semaglutide). Design a sophisticated circular badge logo on a deep dark brown background (#2D1F14).

SYMBOL CONCEPT — one single clean icon that must visually unite the ideas of:
(1) a GLP-1 medical syringe/drop, stylized as a minimalist upward-pointing teardrop or droplet shape
(2) inside or emerging from that droplet: a subtle upward arrow or ascending line, representing personal transformation and weight loss journey
(3) the overall symbol should read as both "a drop of medication" AND "an upward movement / renewal"
The symbol must be simple, geometric, elegant — not complex or cluttered. Think luxury wellness brand, not pharmaceutical.

COLOR PALETTE — strict:
- Background circle: very dark warm brown #2D1F14
- Symbol and all decorative elements: copper/bronze gradient #C4885A to #DCAA78
- App name typography: off-white cream #F5EDE3
- Tagline typography: muted copper #C4885A

TYPOGRAPHY — two lines below the symbol:
- Line 1: "Renove GLP-1" — bold, modern serif or elegant sans-serif, wide letter-spacing, cream color, large and confident
- Line 2: "sua jornada, seu ritmo" — thin/light weight, small size, copper color, italic, centered

COMPOSITION:
- Circular badge format (perfect for app icon and profile photo)
- Thin elegant double-ring border in copper around the outer edge of the badge
- Symbol centered in the upper 55% of the badge
- Typography fills the lower 35%, with a thin horizontal ornamental rule separating symbol from text
- Generous negative space — do not fill every area, let the dark background breathe
- Small decorative dot or diamond accent on the separator line

STYLE REFERENCES: think of luxury wellness brands, premium skincare logos, or high-end medical spa branding — warm, sophisticated, human, not cold or clinical. Similar aesthetic to Chanel, Augustinus Bader, or La Mer but warmer and more approachable.

TECHNICAL: high resolution, vector-clean edges, no gradients on the background (solid dark brown), symbol may have subtle copper gradient, sharp and crisp at any size, transparent background (PNG), 1024x1024 pixels.

DO NOT include: pills, full human figures, scales, before/after imagery, green/blue colors, overly complex illustration, drop shadows, glows, or any elements not described above.
```

## VARIAÇÕES PARA TESTAR (se o primeiro não sair perfeito)

### Variação A — foco no símbolo de gota + seta:
```
Append to main prompt: "The central symbol is specifically: a teardrop shape pointing upward, with a small clean arrow emerging from its tip. The teardrop bottom is rounded and full, tapering to a sharp upward point that transitions into the arrow. The entire symbol reads as ONE unified shape, not two separate elements. Copper color. Minimal. Geometric. No outlines, filled shape only."
```

### Variação B — foco no monograma R estilizado:
```
Append to main prompt: "The central symbol is specifically: a stylized capital letter R where the descending leg is inverted — instead of going down-right, it ascends upward-right at 40 degrees, ending in a small arrowhead. The R bowl is a perfect open circle (not fully closed). At the base of the R stem, a small filled copper circle represents the starting point of the journey. The symbol reads simultaneously as the letter R AND as a path going upward."
```

### Variação C — símbolo abstrato minimalista:
```
Append to main prompt: "The central symbol is specifically: three thin concentric arcs (like a wifi signal or rising sound waves) oriented vertically, growing larger from bottom to top, colored in copper. Beneath them, a small solid circle. Together they represent: waves of weekly treatment building upward into transformation. Ultra minimal, think Nike swoosh level of simplicity."
```
